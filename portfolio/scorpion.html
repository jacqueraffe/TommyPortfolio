Scorpion (2.007)

Sophomore Capstone

Previous Next

The Challenge

For my sophomore design capstone, I was tasked with building a robot to compete in the 2.007 (Design and Manufacturing I) competition. The theme was based on "The Expanse".

The Design

I designed a unique 4-bar linkage claw mechanism optimized for picking up scoring objects with high precision and speed.

The Assembly

The robot features a robust aluminum chassis and custom 3D-printed components. I performed extensive FEA on the claw arms to ensure they could withstand the forces during competition.
