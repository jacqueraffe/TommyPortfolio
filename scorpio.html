Scorpion (2.007)

Sophomore Capstone — Design and Manufacturing I

01. Problem Statement

"Design a robot to interact with the Gooble Box, a 2x2 button panel on a 25° incline. The robot must apply 20N to top buttons and 10N to the bottom..."

MCM: Camshaft Design

Developed a custom camshaft mechanism to convert rotational motor motion into high-frequency linear button presses.

Chassis & Drivetrain

Utilized a narrow wheelbase and tank treads to navigate the incline while maintaining constant contact with the Gooble Box track.
