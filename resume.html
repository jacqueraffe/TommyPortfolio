Who I Am

Hey! My name is Thomas Larsen and I am a Mechanical Engineer (Course 2) at MIT, with a minor in Computer Science (Course 6). I have a passion for precision machine design and bio-inspired robotics. Thanks for coming to check out my portfolio!

I play Alto Saxophone and love jazz-fusion & indie-rock. I am also a co-founder of MIT LIVE, a dynamic music group on campus. We create bands of students with common song interests and put on showcases every semester.

Thomas Larsen, 2024
